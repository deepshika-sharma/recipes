Chickpea Soup

Description

Good for cold days. I especially love soups when I have an upset stomach.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • carrots
  • celery
  • onion
  • garlic
  • chickpeas
  • chicken stock or water
  • salt
  • pepper
  • noodles/pasta of your choice

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to a pan
  2. Add the vegetables and saute for a few minutes
  3. Add the chickpeas and saute for a few mins
  4. Add in the chicken stock or water
  5. Add pepper and salt if required
  6. Bring to a boil and let it simmer until the veggies are soft
  7. Add the pasta/noodles
  8. Cook until noodles have become cooked
